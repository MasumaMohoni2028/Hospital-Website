TERMS Please follow rules.

Patient Confidentiality: Respecting and maintaining the privacy and confidentiality of patients' personal and medical information. Informed Consent: Obtaining the patient's voluntary and informed consent before any medical procedure or treatment, after providing them with all the necessary information regarding the procedure, risks, and benefits. Visiting Hours: Following the designated visiting hours to ensure a conducive environment for patient care and recovery. Visitors should respect any restrictions or limitations imposed by the hospital. No Smoking Policy: Adhering to the hospital's policy of not smoking within the premises, including both indoor and outdoor areas. Noise Control: Maintaining a quiet and peaceful environment within the hospital to facilitate healing and recovery for patients. This involves speaking softly, avoiding unnecessary noise, and adhering to any posted guidelines. Hygiene and Infection Control: Practicing good hand hygiene by washing hands or using hand sanitizers provided. Following any infection control protocols, such as wearing masks or gowns, as instructed by healthcare staff. Restricted Areas: Respecting restricted areas within the hospital that are designated for authorized personnel only. These areas may include operating rooms, intensive care units, or certain administrative areas. Respect for Staff: Showing respect, politeness, and cooperation towards healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and other staff members, while understanding their workload and responsibilities. Payment and Billing: Adhering to the hospital's payment policies, providing accurate information for insurance claims, and settling any financial obligations promptly. Safety Regulations: Following safety guidelines and regulations, such as not tampering with medical equipment, reporting any safety hazards or incidents, and following evacuation procedures in case of emergencies.
